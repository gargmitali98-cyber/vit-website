### Customer Portal – Design Requirements

* Clean, modern and intuitive UX
* Guided step-by-step journey
* Progressive disclosure (show only relevant questions)
* Disease search and selection capability
* Card-based disease categories
* Mobile-first and responsive
* Progress tracker and save draft
* Minimal form fatigue
* Simple language and clear actions
* Focus on fast completion and customer experience

**Keywords:** Simple • Guided • Searchable • Mobile-Friendly • Conversational

---

### Admin / Underwriter Portal – Design Requirements

* Enterprise SaaS experience
* Dashboard-driven interface
* Configuration-first design
* Data tables with search, filters and actions
* Visual rule and questionnaire management
* Case review and application management
* Clear module navigation
* Audit and governance visibility
* Optimized for power users and underwriters

**Keywords:** Enterprise • Configurable • Operational • Data-Driven • Underwriter-Focused

---

### Overall Theme

* Premium InsurTech platform
* Modern healthcare blue palette
* Clean typography and iconography
* Production-ready software experience
* Similar to Guidewire, Duck Creek, Salesforce, ServiceNow

**Customer Portal = Simplicity & Completion**
**Admin Portal = Control & Configuration**
